AIS Speed Anomaly Detection Indicator 10 — GPS spoofing proxy (implied speed > 50 kt)

Scans consecutive AIS position pairs. Any pair implying >50 kt is recorded as an IND10 anomaly and factored into the vessel risk score on next screening.

Open-Water Loitering Detection Indicator 9 — Stationary ≥ 12 h outside anchorage

Groups consecutive AIS positions with SOG < 2 kt into episodes. Episodes ≥ 12 h are recorded as IND9 loitering events. Risk levels: MEDIUM ≥ 12 h · HIGH ≥ 24 h or in a high-risk zone · CRITICAL ≥ 48 h.

Sanctioned Port Call Detection Indicator 29 — Vessel at sanctioned terminal (Iran · Russia · DPRK · Syria · Venezuela)

Geofences AIS positions against ~30 sanctioned terminals. Consecutive in-port positions are collapsed into a single visit record. Matched visits add IND29 weight to the vessel risk score.
